About Me

Hi! I'm Devi Yolanda — but you can just call me Devi. I currently live in Jakarta with my small, loving family, and they’re my biggest source of happiness and motivation every single day. I work at an IT company in a hybrid setup, which is perfect for juggling my roles as a mom and someone who loves working in tech. I’m deeply passionate about learning, especially in the world of technology. In between the beautiful chaos of parenting and my day job, I’ve carved out time to dive into the world of coding — I’ve taught myself programming languages like PHP, Java, and C# — mostly through YouTube and random front-end dev websites I stumbled upon. Eventually, I started building simple websites just for fun — and honestly, it’s been so rewarding seeing my ideas come to life.

For me, coding isn’t just about telling computers what to do. It’s part of my personal journey — growing bit by bit as a mom, a techie, and someone who’s always curious and learning.
